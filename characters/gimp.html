Gimp is an assistant in the game and at time may participate, although its against his will most times and more so made to play as a punishment if he had been deemed misbehaved.. He was rather optimistic but still extremely nervous in a way while in the circus. Even with the circus owner mistreating and abusing staff. Although he wasnt very smart.. but is smart in his own ways? , due to the circus owners making him extremely sheltered in education and certain things everyday people would and should probably know. So he became dependent on seeing and entertaining people due to that being basically the only thing he could really do, he did enjoy it though! The effects of being so sheltered and only really being around children.. had caused some developmental difficulties and his personality was very similar to that of the children he saw. And that had caused him to be extremely vulnerable, naive, gullible.., and very easy to manipulate or fool. Causing him to be used a lot by the circus owners & becoming the main entertainment mascot at his workplace. So with that in mind made him think that all their actions are normal thing that everyone has gone through. He is also very attached to anyone who is even in the slightest way nice towards him due to him having no family [estranged]. He does end up leaving the circus at 16.. in a way. As told.., Gimp’s personality is very different as the average 26 year old because of his upbringing and also the development issues he has.
